How long does it take to build a website or app?

It depends on the project size and features. A simple website can take 1 to 2 weeks, while a custom app with advanced features may take 1 to 3 months.

Do you provide both design and development?

Yes. I handle the full process — from UI/UX design to coding and deployment. I can also work with your team if you only need one part of the process.

Will my website/app work on all devices?

Absolutely! Everything I build is responsive and optimized for desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

Why Good UX is Good Business

A beautiful website or app doesn’t mean much if users can’t find what they need. User Experience (UX) focuses on how easy, fast, and enjoyable it is for people to use your product. Studies show that better UX can double conversion rates because customers prefer websites that are simple and intuitive.
